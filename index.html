Self-Designed Rotary Engine

I designed and modeled my own version of a rotary engine based on extensive research and planning. This project combines my knowledge of metal fabrication, machining, and CAD design.

Materials: A356 Aluminum Alloy (engine block), Hard-Anodized Aluminum (6061-T6, eccentric shaft). The design focuses on optimizing thermal efficiency and mechanical durability, considering real-world manufacturing constraints.

Research Source: "Rotary Engine" by Kenichi Yamamoto (1981). This project highlights my ability to innovate while solving engineering challenges, demonstrating a balance between theoretical understanding and practical application.

Arduino Controlled Robotic Claw

Developed an Arduino-based robotic claw for educational purposes. This project integrates sensors for object detection, servos for movement, and an Arduino microcontroller to control the claw's operations.

Key features include precise control over grip strength, object recognition, and interactive user control via a custom-built interface. The claw can pick up small objects with accuracy, showcasing practical application of embedded systems and robotics.

Cardboard Chair Design

Designed and constructed a functional chair entirely out of cardboard, focusing on sustainability and innovative design. This project explores the potential of common materials in furniture design, emphasizing structural integrity and ergonomic considerations.

The chair was designed to support weight, showcasing the strength of cardboard when properly engineered. The process involved CAD modeling, prototyping, and iterative testing to achieve a balance between aesthetics and functionality.

Adaptive Device for Visually Impaired

Created an adaptive device to assist visually impaired individuals with daily tasks. This project involved designing a haptic feedback system that uses ultrasonic sensors to detect obstacles and provides directional cues through vibration patterns.

The device was developed with user-centered design principles, ensuring it was intuitive and comfortable for users. It includes a wearable component and integrates with a smartphone app for additional features like voice navigation and emergency alerts.
