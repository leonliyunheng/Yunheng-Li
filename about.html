About Me

My name is Leon, and I am a second-year Manufacturing Engineering student at UBC, focused on developing advanced technical skills in modern manufacturing processes. Originally from Shenzhen, China, I moved to Montreal at the age of 13, which provided me with a global perspective and a passion for problem-solving. My hands-on experience with advanced machinery, including CNC and water jet cutters, has strengthened my technical foundation and commitment to the industry. I am eager to apply my skills to contribute to innovative solutions and drive advancements in the manufacturing sector.
